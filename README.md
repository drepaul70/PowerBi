 ## 2. SOFT DRINKS ANALYSIS
 ## OVERVIEW
This Soft Drink Market Insights Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of 1,500 retail outlets, highlighting Coca-Cola as the market leader in both sales and visibility. While PET bottles dominate consumer preference, a significant operational gap exists with only 39.67% of outlets being well-stocked. The data suggests a strategic focus on improving restocking frequency and leveraging cross-purchase behavior (like Coke and Fanta bundles) to optimize market performance.

## 4. HEALTHCARE ANALYSIS DASHBOARD
## OVERVIEW
The Healthcare Analysis Dashboard tracks 40,235 patients across 32,783 hospitals, with an average patient age of 52 and a 16-day average stay. Medical conditions like Arthritis and Diabetes are the most prevalent, with patient admissions remaining relatively steady throughout the year, peaking slightly in August. Gender distribution is nearly equal, and test results are distributed evenly across Abnormal, Normal, and Inconclusive categories.
